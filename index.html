About Me

Hi! My name is Scott.
I'm a double master's student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor! I'm an electrical and computer engineering major in the signal and image processing and machine learning track and a biomedical engineering major in the bioelectrics and neural engineering track. My goal is to join a company that combines machine learning and 3D printing to create advanced and easily reproducible neural and myoelectric prosthetics.
I hope to be at the forefront of artificial intelligence capabilities and to utilize them to make the world a better place for those who are less fortunate. My inspiration came in 2012 when I watched Nigel Ackland showing off his new prosthetic, the "terminator" arm.
